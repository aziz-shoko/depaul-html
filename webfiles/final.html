About Us

Welcome to Hot and Fresh premiun coffee shop, where each cup of coffee is more than just a drink, its an experience! Our coffee shop was born from a simple dream, to create a haven for coffee lovers and a community space for everyone to enjoy the experience together!

Our journey began in 2023, with a passionate team of coffee enthsiasts who believed in the power of well brewed cup of coffee to transform a day. We get our ingredients from the finest organic grown bean farms around the world, where every sip you take is not only delicious but also sustainably and ethically produced.

At Hot and Fresh premium coffee, we pride ourselves on our exquisite approach. Each cup is crafted with the utmost care, using time-honored brewing methods that bring out the unique flavors and aromoas of our organic beans!
